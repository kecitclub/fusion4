Zero Waste Meals

"Waste Less, Feed More!"


As per a United Nations report, an average Nepali individual wastes 79 kilogrammes of food annually. This rate surpasses those of both India and China. In Nepal, food waste occurs in homes owing, inter alia, to inadequacy of storage facilities. Food loss occurs in the production, distribution and storage stages. Food wastage is a significant, not only in Nepal, global issue. We aim to solve this issue by creating a platform where diverse groups of people can integrate and collaborate.
